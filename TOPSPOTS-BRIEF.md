# TopSpots — Complete Product Brief for Landing Page Design

## What is TopSpots?

TopSpots is a B2B2C venue discovery and management platform built for the hospitality industry in Poland and Europe. It serves two audiences: venue owners/managers and hospitality staff (bartenders, waiters, chefs, hosts, baristas, managers).

**Live app**: https://topspots.global

### FAQ (6 Questions)

**Q: How long does the approval process take?**
A: Most venues are approved within 24 hours. We review your submission to ensure quality and accuracy.

**Q: Do I need any special hardware?**
A: No. TopSpots works entirely through web browsers and QR codes. No POS integration, tablets, or special equipment needed.

**Q: Can I customize my venue's profile?**
A: Absolutely. You control your photos, menu, description, opening hours, and more. Your profile is your digital storefront.

**Q: How does the tipping system work?**
A: Customers scan a QR code at your venue to tip staff directly. Tips are processed digitally — no cash handling needed. You can track all tip activity in your dashboard.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**
A: No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: The platform supports 8 languages: English, Polish, Ukrainian, German, Spanish, French, Italian, and Portuguese. Both the interface and your venue content can be multilingual.

## TECHNICAL DETAILS

### Platform Architecture (from audit)
- **68 Supabase tables** with full RLS (Row Level Security)
- **18 Supabase Edge Functions** for serverless backend logic
- **4 user roles**: super_admin, admin (venue owner), staff, user (customer)
- **8 languages**: EN, PL, UK, DE, ES, FR, IT, PT
- **Payment**: Stripe integration for subscriptions and tipping
- **Auth**: Supabase Auth with email/password and social login

### Current App Stack
- React 18 + TypeScript + Vite
- Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui + Radix UI
- Supabase (DB, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions)
- Framer Motion for animations
- React Router for navigation
- i18next for internationalization

## COMPETITOR DESIGN REFERENCES

Best-in-class landing pages in this space (study these for inspiration):

1. **SevenRooms** (sevenrooms.com) — Premium dark navy + gold aesthetic, outcome-first headlines, $4B revenue stat, segmented by venue type. Closest to TopSpots brand.
2. **Resy OS** (resy.com/os) — Sophisticated, editorial feel. "Turn Reservations into Relationships." Tabbed features. Consultation-style CTA.
3. **Toast** (pos.toasttab.com) — Warm orange, product-led storytelling, interactive demos. Strong for showing the product in action.
4. **7shifts** (7shifts.com) — Manager/Employee toggle for features. Purple + green. "Building better restaurant teams one shift at a time."
5. **Deputy** (deputy.com) — Segmented by business size. Blue, corporate. Strong trust badges (G2, Capterra, Gartner).

### Key Design Patterns to Follow
- Outcome-first headlines (revenue/time saved, not features)
- Dual CTA in hero (primary action + secondary exploration)
- Bento grid for features (largest card = most important feature)
- Layered social proof (stats → logos → testimonials → review badges)
- Dark hero sections for premium feel
- Generous whitespace (80-120px between sections)
- Subtle scroll-triggered animations
- "No credit card required" on every trial CTA
- 3-tier pricing with one highlighted as "Most Popular"
- Mobile-first with sticky bottom CTA
